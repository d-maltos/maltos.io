ASF HEATMAPS PROGRAM

The ASF Heatmaps Program was an innovative project that my team and I designed at the University of Alaska Fairbanks during our Data Engineering course. As students, Lisa Jacklin, Yali Wang, and myself, embarked on a journey to transform the abundant Sentinel-1 satellite data archived by the Alaska Satellite Facility into an informative two-dimensional heatmap. This heatmap became a crucial tool, illustrating the geophysical footprint of satellite coverage and providing a visual narrative of the variations in data density and availability across diverse regions.
